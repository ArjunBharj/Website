Homemade Peanut Sauce

This is a test to see if i can make a website dedicated to my peanut sauce recipe.

Here's what you'll need

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3 tbs tahini
  • 3 tbs peanut butter smooth
  • 1 tbs rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbs Sriracha
  • 2cm of ginger

note: be generous with these portions

Follow these next steps

  1. mix all ingredients in a bowl
  2. pour water into bowl until you attain the desired consistency of the sauce

note: water does not affect the tast of the sauce.
